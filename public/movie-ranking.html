The Best Movies According to Dheeraj Kumar

My Top 3 Movies of all-Times.

The Spider-Man

Spider-Man: Homecoming (2017) is a Marvel superhero film that follows Peter Parker (played by Tom Holland), a high school student who is also the superhero Spider-Man. After his appearance in Captain America: Civil War, Peter returns home to continue his life as a teenager, balancing school and crime-fighting. He is eager to prove himself to Tony Stark (Iron Man), but his chance comes when he uncovers a dangerous weapons operation led by the villainous Vulture (played by Michael Keaton). Peter must face his own insecurities and responsibilities, while learning what it truly means to be a hero. The film focuses on Peter's growth and his relationship with Tony Stark, exploring his struggle to find his place in the larger Marvel Universe.

Thor

Thor (2011) is a Marvel superhero film that introduces the Norse god of thunder, Thor (played by Chris Hemsworth). The movie follows Thor, a powerful yet arrogant warrior, who is banished from his home in the mystical realm of Asgard to Earth as punishment by his father, Odin (played by Anthony Hopkins). While on Earth, Thor learns humility and responsibility as he teams up with scientist Jane Foster (Natalie Portman) and her team to stop his adoptive brother, Loki (Tom Hiddleston), who is plotting to take the throne of Asgard and unleash chaos.

Iron-Man

Iron Man (2008) is the film that kick-started the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). It follows Tony Stark (played by Robert Downey Jr.), a genius billionaire, and playboy who is the CEO of Stark Industries. While in Afghanistan demonstrating his company's latest weaponry, Tony is captured by terrorists and forced to build a weapon for them. Instead, he constructs a suit of armor to escape.
Once back home, Tony begins to reevaluate his life and the impact of his company's weapons on the world. He upgrades the suit, becomes the superhero "Iron Man," and sets out to stop the villainous Obadiah Stane (Jeff Bridges), who is plotting to use a similar suit for his own agenda.
The film focuses on Tony's transformation from a self-centered inventor to a responsible hero. It combines action, humor, and technological innovation, making it a pivotal entry in the MCU and solidifying Robert Downey Jr. as an iconic superhero lead.
